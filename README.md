## Overview

Ever wonder what your employees are secretly feeding ChatGPT, Bard, or other AI tools at work? 🤔

## 🛡️ Mitigation Layers (Prevent, Detect, Correct)

- **Preventive:**  
  - Policies prohibiting sensitive data sharing with unauthorized AI tools 🛑  
  - Employee training and awareness 🧠  

- **Detective:**  
  - Monitor network logs for unknown AI tool access 👀  
  - Flag suspicious activity for review 🚨  

- **Corrective:**  
  - Incident response workflow for policy violations 📝  
  - Logging, escalation, and risk review 🔍
